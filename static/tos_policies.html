Refund Policy

Due to the nature of the products we sell we cannot accept returns or issue refunds after we ship the products. All sales are final.

You may cancel your order if it hadn't ship yet. To cancel an order please send an email to store@aarddict.org. Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.

If you cancel your order after we received your payment, but before we shipped the order we will issue a full refund.

Defective media can be exchanged if claimed within 30 days of shipping date. Defective flash cards must be returned to us before we can send a replacement (customer pays return shipping cost).

Shipping policy

All orders are shipped with USPS, usually within 3-4 business days.

We currently ship to US, Canada, Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Gibraltar, Greece, Guernsey, Iceland, Ireland, Isle of Man, Italy, Jersey, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Monaco, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, San Marino, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and United Kingdom.

7% sales tax will be added to orders shipped to New Jersey.
