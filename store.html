When placing an order in Aard Dictionary Store, keep in mind the following:

  • Wikipedia for Aard Dictionary in various languages available through aarddict.org strives to achieve highest possible accuracy and rendering quality of online articles from corresponding language Wikipedia websites. However, it is not the goal to replicate 100% of the content and appearance of wikipedia.org sites. Articles may contain formatting errors and omissions, with certain features removed deliberately to reduce size of resulting Aard Dictionary files. All the files available for purchase on media or for download are provided as is, without any warranty of any kind. Downloading and carefully examining 10,000 article samples is strongly recommended before placing order.
  • All sales are final, no refunds, no returns, defective media exchange only.
  • Defective media can be exchanged if claimed within 30 days of shipping date.
  • Defective flash cards must be returned to us before replacement is shipped.
  • All orders are shipped with USPS, usually within 3-4 business days.
  • SD and MicroSD cards are high capacity cards and thus require SDHC-compatible device.
  • Information you provide when placing an order in Aard Dictionary store will only be used to process your order. We won't sell it to anyone or share it with any third parties for any purposes other then processing your order.
  • Write to store@aarddict.org if you have any questions, concerns or suggestions.
  • If language or media you're interested in is not listed here please let us know — we may be able to accomodate your request.
